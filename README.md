# 📊 India CPI Inflation Analysis using Advanced Excel

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes **India's Consumer Price Index (CPI) Inflation** using **Advanced Microsoft Excel** to identify inflation trends, category-wise contributions, the impact of COVID-19, food inflation drivers, and the relationship between crude oil prices and CPI inflation.

The analysis demonstrates how Excel's analytical features can be used to transform raw economic data into meaningful insights through data visualization and statistical analysis.


## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze historical CPI inflation trends in India.
* Identify major contributors to overall inflation.
* Study the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic on inflation.
* Examine food inflation and its key drivers.
* Explore the correlation between crude oil prices and CPI inflation.
* Present insights using interactive dashboards and charts.

## 📈 Key Analysis Performed

### 1. CPI Inflation Trend Analysis

* Analyzed monthly and yearly inflation patterns.
* Identified periods of high and low inflation.

### 2. Category-wise Inflation Contribution

* Compared the contribution of various CPI categories.
* Visualized category weights using Pivot Charts and Pie Charts.

### 3. COVID-19 Impact Analysis

* Compared inflation levels before, during, and after the pandemic.
* Highlighted major economic shifts during COVID-19.

### 4. Food Inflation Analysis

* Examined food price inflation trends.
* Identified categories with the highest inflation impact.

### 5. Crude Oil vs CPI Inflation

* Performed correlation analysis between crude oil prices and CPI inflation.
* Evaluated how fluctuations in oil prices influence overall inflation.
